About Face-Ticket
Face-Ticket is a secure facial recognition system that authenticates students at the exam hall. It enhances fairness by preventing unauthorized impersonation.
How It Works
1. Admin registers student faces.
2. Student is scanned at entry.
3. Face is verified.
4. Attendance is marked or fraud flagged.
